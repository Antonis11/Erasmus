WHAT OPPORTUNITIES DOES IT GIVE TO STUDENTS?

Erasmus+ enables students enrolled in a Higher Education Institution, at all levels of study (undergraduate, postgraduate, doctorate) to move to study at a partner institution abroad with full recognition for the duration of their studies. the opportunity to carry out an Internship in a host institution abroad with full recognition for the duration of their work.

WHAT OPPORTUNITIES DOES IT GIVE TO THE TEACHING AND ADMINISTRATIVE STAFF OF INSTITUTIONS?

Erasmus+ gives the opportunity to the Teaching and Administrative staff of Higher Education Institutions to move respectively for teaching/training and training in institutions abroad.
